How it works

Simple. Fast. Rewarding.

STEP 01

Join your group

Add @whisky_sour_bot to your Telegram group. Register with /start and connect your Solana wallet.

STEP 02

Enter a round

Type /play to enter the next round. Pay 0.05 SOL entry. Rounds run every 5 minutes.

STEP 03

Win or earn tickets

80% of players win a share of the loser pool. Losers earn +2 jackpot tickets. Winners earn +1.

STEP 04

Hit the jackpot

Every 288 rounds (daily), one player wins the jackpot. More tickets = better odds.

Game Mechanics

The math is on your side.

Entry Fee

0.05 SOL per round. Minimum 3 players required or full refund.

Winners (80%)

Split the losers' 25% pool equally. Everyone gets the same share.

Losers (20%)

Lose only 25% of entry (0.0125 SOL). Earn +2 jackpot tickets.

Fee Split

1% jackpot fund. 0.75% group owner. 0.25% dev.

Jackpot System

Losing builds your edge.

Win a round +1 ticket
Lose a round +2 tickets
Max tickets 50 tickets
Jackpot draw Every 288 rounds (daily)
Winner selection Weighted random by tickets
After jackpot All tickets reset
